Our Mission

EcoTrack Rwanda is dedicated to transforming plastic waste management in Rwanda through innovative tracking, community engagement, and reward systems. We aim to reduce plastic pollution by 50% in the next 5 years while creating economic opportunities for local communities.

Reduce Waste

Minimize plastic waste ending up in landfills and natural environments through proper sorting and recycling.

Community Engagement

Empower local communities with knowledge and tools to participate in plastic waste management.

Reward System

Incentivize recycling through our Eco-points system that can be redeemed for valuable rewards.

Plastic Sorting Guide

Learn how to properly sort your plastic waste to maximize recycling efficiency and earn more Eco-points.

PET

Polyethylene Terephthalate - Used for water bottles, soda bottles.

Rinse and remove caps
Flatten to save space
2

HDPE

High-Density Polyethylene - Used for milk jugs, shampoo bottles.

Rinse thoroughly
Remove labels if possible
3

PVC

Polyvinyl Chloride - Used for pipes, credit cards.

Check local guidelines
Separate from other plastics
4

LDPE

Low-Density Polyethylene - Used for plastic bags, squeeze bottles.

Bundle bags together
Clean and dry

Recycling Tips

Clean Before Recycling

Rinse containers to remove food residue. This prevents contamination of the recycling stream.

Non-Recyclable Items

Plastic straws, utensils, and styrofoam are not recyclable in most programs.

Check Local Guidelines

Recycling capabilities vary by location. Check with your local facility for specifics.

Sort Properly

Separate plastics by type to maximize the value of recycled materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EcoTrack Rwanda and plastic recycling.

You earn Eco-points by bringing sorted plastic waste to any of our collection points. Points are awarded based on the type and weight of plastic you recycle. For example, 1kg of PET bottles earns you 20 points, while 1kg of HDPE earns 15 points.

You can find all our collection points on the interactive map in the "Collection Points" section of our website. We have locations in Kigali, Rubavu, Musanze, Huye, and other major towns across Rwanda.

We accept all types of plastic marked with recycling symbols 1 through 7. This includes PET (1), HDPE (2), PVC (3), LDPE (4), PP (5), PS (6), and other plastics (7). Please refer to our Sorting Guide for detailed instructions on how to prepare each type.

Once you've accumulated enough points, visit the Rewards section of our website or app. Browse the available rewards and click "Redeem Now" on the item you want. You'll receive instructions on how to claim your reward, which may involve picking it up at a collection center or having it delivered.

EcoTrack Rwanda is creating a circular economy for plastics in Rwanda. By incentivizing proper recycling, we're:

  • Reducing plastic pollution in our environment
  • Conserving natural resources by reusing materials
  • Reducing greenhouse gas emissions from plastic production
  • Creating green jobs in the recycling sector
  • Educating communities about sustainable waste management
